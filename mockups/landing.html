The Three Pillars

Simple by design.
Profound by nature.

Three movements that define every exchange inside the circle. Read them slowly — they are also the rules.

01 — Verify

You're welcomed, not signed up.

Apply for a seat. We verify every neighbour personally — by call, often by visit — to keep the circle small enough that trust holds.

02 — Give

Your hour earns a credit.

Share an hour of a skill you love. One Samay Credit lands in your account. Your expertise — whatever it is — has the same weight as everyone else's.

03 — Receive

Spend it on any other hour.

A web designer can receive home cooking. A tutor can receive plant care. Inside the circle, time is the great equaliser.

The Ethics

An hour is an hour. Whether you are designing a website or walking a neighbour's dog, the value of your time remains equal. We trade in humanity, not hierarchy.
