The Three Movements

Simple by design.
Profound by nature.

01 — Verify

You're welcomed, not signed up.

Apply for a seat. We verify every neighbour personally — by call, often by visit — so the circle stays small enough that trust holds.

02 — Give

Your hour earns a credit.

Share an hour of a skill you love. One Samay Credit lands in your account. Your expertise — whatever it is — weighs the same as everyone else's.

03 — Receive

Spend it on any other hour.

A web designer can receive home cooking. A tutor can receive plant care. Inside the circle, time is the great equaliser.
