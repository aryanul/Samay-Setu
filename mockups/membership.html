The basics

Completely. No money ever changes hands between members, and there's no subscription. The only currency is time: one hour given earns one credit, redeemable for any hour offered in the circle.

Almost any hour of help — professional skills (tax, legal, design, tutoring), home and care (cooking, elder check-ins, childcare, repairs), knowledge, and craft. If it's a legal, safe hour of your time, it belongs here.

Then you're exactly who this is for. Cooking, listening, gardening, walking a dog, sitting with an elder — every hour is equal. There is no hierarchy of skill in Samay Setu.

Credits & fairness

Because pricing one person's hour above another's is exactly the inequality we set out to remove. A surgeon's hour and a student's hour are both one credit. Equality isn't a slogan here — it's built into the math.

No. Credits can only be earned by giving your time, and they can't be converted to money. This keeps value circulating inside the neighbourhood instead of leaking out of it.

Founding Members start with five credits already in their account, so you can receive help before you've given any. It's our thank-you for helping build the first circle.

Trust & safety

Every member is verified personally before joining — by call and often an in-person visit. The circle is intentionally small and capped, so accountability is real and people genuinely know one another.

Credits. You can only receive an hour if you hold a credit, and credits are only earned by giving. Every exchange is logged and confirmed by both sides, so reputation is built on real, completed hours.

It stays yours. We don't run ads and we don't sell your information. We collect only what's needed to verify you and run the circle — nothing more.

Joining

Apply using the form above. We'll reach out within two days for a short verification call, and if it's a fit, a seat opens. We add members in small waves to keep the circle healthy.
