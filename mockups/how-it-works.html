Home  /  How it works

The whole idea in four steps

Give an hour.
Earn a credit.
Spend it anywhere.

No money moves inside Samay Setu. The only thing that changes hands is time — and every hour is worth exactly one hour, no matter whose it is.

1

Apply, and get verified.

You don't sign up — you're welcomed in. Tell us who you are and what you'd love to share. We verify every neighbour personally by call, and often with a short visit, before a seat opens.

Takes
A short call within 48 hours of applying.
Why
A small, verified circle is what keeps trust real.
2

Offer an hour of something you love.

List a skill — tax filing, tutoring, cooking, plant care, anything. When a neighbour books it and you complete the hour, one Samay Credit lands in your account. That's it.

You earn
1 credit for every hour you give.
Day one
Founding members start with a 5-hour Trust Bonus.
3

Spend it on any hour you need.

Your credit isn't tied to your trade. The designer who gave an hour can receive home cooking; the cook can receive a tutoring session. Browse the circle, request an hour, and spend a credit.

1 credit
= any one hour offered in the circle.
No expiry games
Credits are yours to spend when you need them.
4

The bridge holds itself up.

Every completed hour leaves a small, honest record both sides can see. Over time the circle becomes a living web of who helped whom — the quiet accountability that lets strangers become neighbours.

After each hour
Both people confirm it happened. The ledger updates.
Result
Reputation built on hours, not reviews-for-hire.

The Samay Credit

One currency,
everyone equal.

  • 01
    It's earned, not bought

    You can only get credits by giving your time. Money can't buy them and they can't be cashed out.

  • 02
    It's flat

    A surgeon's hour and a student's hour are both one credit. The platform refuses to price one person above another.

  • 03
    It circulates

    Credits move through the circle, not into a company's pocket. Value stays inside the neighbourhood.

Why it stays safe

Trust isn't a feature.
It's the architecture.

Verified, one by one

No anonymous sign-ups. Every member is a real, confirmed neighbour before they ever appear in the circle.

A small, capped circle

We grow in waves, not floods. A bounded community means people actually know — and answer to — one another.

An honest ledger

Every exchange is logged and mutually confirmed. Reputation is earned through completed hours, never bought.
